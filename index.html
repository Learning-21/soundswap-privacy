Privacy Policy — Sound Swap

Last updated: June 26, 2026
Contact: tylerjohnrowan@gmail.com

Overview

Sound Swap helps children practice phonological awareness by listening to word pairs and identifying which sound changed.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we store:

  • Display name (for greeting on the home screen)
  • Email address (for login)
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash — never plain text)
  • Game progress (levels completed, scores, streaks)

The app does not require a microphone. Word audio is played from bundled recordings or text-to-speech when a recording is missing.

How We Use Information

We use account information only to sign you in, save progress, and improve the learning experience. We do not sell personal information. We do not show third-party advertising.

Children's Privacy

Sound Swap is designed for children ages 4–8 with adult supervision. Accounts should be created by a parent, guardian, or educator. We collect only the minimum information needed to save progress.

Data Storage

Account and progress data may be stored on your device and, when enabled, on our cloud database so progress can sync across devices.

Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your name, email, password, and all saved progress.
